Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

ScanPro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the ScanPro mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

ScanPro processes your documents, PDFs, and images locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your personal files or documents to any external server. We may collect anonymous usage analytics (crash reports, feature usage) to improve the app.

3. Camera & Storage Permissions

The app requests camera access solely for scanning documents, and storage access to read and save PDF files on your device. These permissions are never used for any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

ScanPro may use third-party SDKs such as Google Play Services for app distribution and analytics. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not sell your data to any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
